Our Story

Reigns Children's home was founded in 2010 with the vision of providing vulnerable children a safe space where they can grow with dignity and love.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To narture, educate, and empower vulnerable children.

Vision: A world where every child has a safe and loving home.

What We Do

  • Provide education and life skills training.
  • Offer counselling and healthcare
  • Organize recreational and cultural activities.
